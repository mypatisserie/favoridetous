About Us

Favori de Tous Pastry

At Patisserie, we relish the process of making pastries. Our baked goods are lovingly made by hand, pastries made in a traditional way and we only use wholesome ingredients with no preservative or extenders. All our recipes were developed with the utmost care as these were made with our families in mind. We look forward to baking for you and your family!
